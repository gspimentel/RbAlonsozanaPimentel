The Impact of Hallyu



It’s obvious that the Hallyu Wave has affected the entire world in more ways than we can imagine: for instance, South Korea has experienced economic growth due to the rise of tourists attracted to the country, its culture, and famous landmarks such as the Namsan Tower and the Gyeongbokgung Palace, both of which are heavily present in K-Dramas and K-Films. It’s also easy to see that the Hallyu Wave has made its way in our everyday lives, from listening to catchy music to binge-watching the famous series and movies.

However, there are also other effects that have changed our ways of thinking. Some of these, as mentioned by Obice (2021), are “It’s okay for guys to be 'feminine'”, “Nothing This Good Comes Without Hard Work”, and that “Asian Talent” is a force to be reckoned with. So, in a way, we can thank this Wave for reminding us about the important lessons we sometimes fail to consider in our everyday lives.
